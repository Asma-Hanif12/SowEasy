# 🌿 SowEasy

**SowEasy** is a smart plant care application designed to help users keep their plants healthy with personalized reminders, care tracking, and organized schedules. Whether you're caring for a single houseplant or a growing collection, SowEasy makes plant care simple, consistent, and stress-free.

## ✨ Features

### 🌱 Plant Management
- Add & Manage Multiple Plants
- Personalized Plant Profiles
- Care History Tracking
- Plant Collection Organization

### 💧 Smart Care Reminders
- Watering Notifications
- Fertilizing Reminders
- Routine Maintenance Alerts
- Custom Care Schedules

### 📅 Task Management
- Create Custom Care Tasks
- Daily Care Schedule
- Mark Tasks as Completed
- Activity Tracking

### 🔔 Notifications
- Timely Care Reminders
- Personalized Alerts
- Recurring Notifications

## 🚀 Why SowEasy?

SowEasy helps plant lovers stay organized and never miss an important care task. With personalized reminders and easy-to-manage schedules, the app promotes healthier plants and makes plant care enjoyable for beginners and experienced gardeners alike.
